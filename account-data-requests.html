Account & Data Requests

DayTripping, published by Waltco

This page explains how to request deletion of your DayTripping account and the personal data associated with it, and how to make other privacy requests (such as access, correction, or export of your data).

How to request account & data deletion

To request deletion of your account and the personal data associated with it, email us at:

Email: waltcoappdev@gmail.com

Please send your request:

  • From the email address associated with your account, so we can verify ownership.
  • With the subject line “Delete my account”.

We will process your request and delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When we delete your account, we remove your account information (including your email address and login credentials) and the personal data tied to your account, such as your personal itinerary items, packing lists and outfit plans, and your push notification token.

What may remain

Content you contributed to a shared trip — such as proposals or shared lodging and transport details — may remain visible to other members of that trip after your account is deleted, because it forms part of their trip records.

Other privacy requests

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the same address:

Email: waltcoappdev@gmail.com

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

More information

For full details on what we collect and how we use it, see our Privacy Policy.
